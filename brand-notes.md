# ARISE Brand Guidelines

## Couleurs (Palette révisée)

| Couleur | Hex (approximatif) | Usage |
|---------|-------------------|-------|
| Teal foncé (Primary) | #1D4E4E | Fond principal, header, footer |
| Teal clair (Sage) | #7A9A8E | Accents secondaires |
| Jaune doré (Gold) | #D4A84B | Boutons CTA, accents |
| Noir/Charcoal | #2D2D2D | Texte principal |
| Terracotta/Coral | #D4856A | Accents warm |

## Typographie

- **Primary typeface**: Open Sans
- **Heading**: Open Sans Bold
- **Subheading**: Open Sans Semibold
- **Body Copy**: Open Sans Regular
- **Emphasis**: Open Sans Italic / Semicondensed

## Logo

- Swirl modernisé symbolisant humanité, croissance et empowerment
- Mouvement ascendant
- Texte: "ARISE HUMAN CAPITAL"

## Brand Personality

- People-centric: Authentic, Empathetic, Uplifting, Grounded
- Growth-focused: Empowering, Transparent, Forward-thinking, Results-oriented

## Tone of Voice

- Warm & Open
- Real & Positive
